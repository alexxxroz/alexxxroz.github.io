About me

I am a master student at the University of Minnesota studying Geographic Information Science . Being a geo data scientist I research, model and analyse terrestrial ecosystems using various geospatial data (including remote sensing) and machine learning approaches. My particular interests include fusion of multispectral imagery and novel computer vision techniques and generating of big geospatial datasets.
